# Vento Sales Plan 2026

## Products
- **Vento Starter**: $99/mo. Includes basic agent building features.
- **Vento Pro**: $299/mo. Includes advanced analytics, unlimited agents, and priority support.
- **Vento Enterprise**: Custom pricing. Includes dedicated account manager and on-premise deployment.

## Sales Goals
- Q1: Acquire 100 new Starter customers.
- Q2: Upgrade 20 Starter customers to Pro.
- Q3: Close 5 Enterprise deals.
- Q4: Reach $1M in ARR.

## Target Audience
- Small to medium businesses looking to automate customer support.
- Large enterprises needing custom internal knowledge agents.

## Sales Strategies
- Outbound email campaigns targeting IT directors.
- Inbound marketing through blog posts and webinars on AI automation.
- Partnerships with tech consultancies.
