# Scenario: HR Feedback and Burnout Analysis

**Description:** Simulate an HR cycle discussing Ana from Marketing, who is feeling burnt out due to Q3 campaign pressure.

## Event Log

**[Ana (Marketing)]**:
"Thank you for meeting with me. Honestly, I'm struggling. The Q3 campaign, especially the 'Revitalize' push targeting the Gen Z demographic on TikTok, feels completely overwhelming. I'm working late every night, even weekends, and I'm worried about the quality of content coming out of it - especially the scheduled posts for next week."

**[HR Business Partner]**:
"Ana, thank you for sharing that. I'm hearing concerns about workload and potential content quality issues for 'Revitalize,' especially within Mesa 12. Let's dig deeper into the specific tasks causing the overload and see if we can correlate this with the documented hours for Lote 8990."

**[Marketing Director]**:
"Ana, thanks for raising this. Let's specifically discuss the Gen Z TikTok content pipeline for 'Revitalize' - Mesa 12. Can we review the tasks assigned to you within Lote 8990, particularly concerning Extrusora 03's output metrics, so we can understand the bottleneck?"

**[Ana (Marketing)]**:
"Okay, so for Mesa 12 and Lote 8990, I'm responsible for scripting, shooting, and editing all TikTok videos related to Extrusora 03's product demos. The metrics show Extrusora 03 requires three new videos per week to hit our reach goals on TikTok, which is just impossible for one person alongside my other duties."

**[HR Business Partner]**:
Okay, Ana, thanks for clarifying. That level of video production, three demos per week solely handled by you within Mesa 12, Lote 8990, for Extrusora 03, does seem unsustainable. Let's explore options – Marketing Director, could we potentially redistribute some of these Extrusora 03 responsibilities within Lote 8990 or temporarily shift resources to alleviate Ana's workload?

**[Marketing Director]**:
Okay, let's look at shifting resources. Ana, while your work in Mesa 12, Lote 8990, Extrusora 03, has been valuable, we can temporarily reassign the scripting for one of those weekly Extrusora 03 TikTok videos to Ben from the product demo team, tracked under sub-task 003B within Lote 8990, starting next week to relieve some pressure. We'll also review overall Lote 8990 budget to potentially bring in freelance video editors specifically for Extrusora 03 demos.
